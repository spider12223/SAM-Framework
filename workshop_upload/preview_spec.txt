===============================================================================
  PREVIEW THUMBNAIL SPEC  —  S.A.M Framework Workshop item
===============================================================================
Give this file to any AI image tool (Midjourney, DALL-E, SDXL, Firefly, etc.)
or a human artist. Goal: a 512x512 icon that matches the S.A.M Mod Builder's
"illuminated manuscript / dark fantasy" look so the Workshop page feels of a
piece with the tool.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HARD REQUIREMENTS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * Dimensions .......... 512 x 512 pixels, exactly square.
  * Format .............. PNG (JPG acceptable). No transparency needed;
                          fill the whole square.
  * Safe area ........... Keep the main emblem + title within the centre 85%.
                          Steam crops/rounds corners on some views.
  * Readable at 64px .... It must still read as "S.A.M" when shrunk to a tiny
                          Workshop tile. Big shapes, high contrast, few words.
  * File size ........... Under 1 MB.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COLOR PALETTE  (copy these hex values — they are the GUI's exact colors)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Background stone (darkest) .... #1a1208
  Background wood (panel) ....... #2a1f0e
  Deep well / inset ............. #211808
  Gold (primary) ................ #d4a84b
  Gold bright (highlights) ...... #f0cd7a
  Gold dim (muted) .............. #8a6d2e
  Parchment (light text) ........ #e8d5a3
  Border / frame ................ #4a3617
  Accent red (sparing, optional). #a03327

  Mood: warm candle-lit gold on near-black brown. NOT neon, NOT cool blues.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMPOSITION
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1. BACKGROUND: dark stone/aged-parchment texture with a subtle radial glow
     (lighter warm brown at top-center fading to near-black at the edges), like
     a torch lighting an old tome. A faint vignette.
  2. FRAME: an ornate thin GOLD border just inside the edges, with a small
     diamond ornament ( a "◆" shape ) at each of the four corners or at the
     top-center. This mirrors the app's «◆ TITLE ◆» panel headers.
  3. CENTRAL EMBLEM (the hero element — pick ONE):
       (a) A stylized gold monogram "S.A.M" set as a heraldic crest, OR
       (b) An anvil-and-quill / gear-and-scroll motif (forging + writing =
           "make mods without code"), OR
       (c) A shield split into three quadrants showing a tiny sword, a helm,
           and a monster eye (class + item + monster), unified by a gold border.
     Whichever you choose: gold linework on the dark ground, softly embossed,
     with a faint inner glow.
  4. TITLE TEXT: "S.A.M" large, in an engraved small-caps SERIF face
     (think Palatino / Trajan / Cinzel), gold (#d4a84b) with a bright top edge
     (#f0cd7a) and a soft dark drop shadow.
  5. SUBTITLE: "SUPPORT ALL MODS" in small letter-spaced small-caps beneath,
     in muted gold (#8a6d2e). Optional tiny line: "a Barony modding framework".

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STYLE KEYWORDS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  dark fantasy, illuminated manuscript, medieval heraldry, engraved gold leaf,
  candle-lit, ornate but clean, game icon, emblem/crest, roguelike, Barony-esque
  pixel-adjacent but rendered smooth. High craft, low clutter.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AVOID (negative prompt)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  neon, cyberpunk, blue/purple glow, photorealistic faces, modern UI chrome,
  cartoon mascots, busy backgrounds, lens flare, text errors / gibberish text,
  watermarks, more than ~5 words of text, low contrast, washed-out grays.
